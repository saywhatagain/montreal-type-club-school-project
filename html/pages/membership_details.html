MEMBERSHIP DETAILS


By becoming a member of the Type Directors Club, you enable us to educate others about type, to celebrate the masters and up-and-coming leaders in the typographic world, and create events to unite those working in or interested in the field of type and typography.

Source: Wikipedia



MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS


THE MONTREAL TYPE CLUB ANNUAL

Each year the Club publishes its annual: Typography. This beautiful hard-bound book is designed by a different award winning design firm every year. The book showcases the winning entries from the MTC’s typography competitions: Communication Design & Typeface Design.

COMPETITION DISCOUNTS

Discounted entry and hanging fees in the Type Club Annual competitions. This is the biggest financial benefit to becoming a member, as members can save hundreds of dollars on competition entry fees if entering multiple pieces.

EVENTS AND SPECIAL CLASSES

Nearly free admission to select MTC events and big discounts on classes and Master Classes. Our members are a wonderful community of people and we pride ourselves on our high quality events and programs.

LIVE STREAMING VIDEOS

Access to live-streaming videos of select events for those unable to attend an event in person. The Cub is building its Video Archive and members have access to all videos as they become available.

RESSOURCES

All members receive access to the MTC Archive and Library.
